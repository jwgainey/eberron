Shifter

Shifters have a little more variation to them, depending on their lycropanthic heritage. They all share the ability to change between their humanoid and animalistic forms once per long or short rest (level + Con in temp HP), have darkvision to 60ft, and have the normal languages from their home. Their humanoid appearances tend towards Human, Half-Orc and Half-Elf, while the animal side varies.

Warforged

Warforged were built with specific jobs in mind. This is something to keep in mind when creating one wether its to keep the thematics in line, or to subvert them. The UA version, had subtypes, but the official release is simply flexible, similarly to a human. All Warforged have +1 AC and the ability to incorporate armor into their bodies.
