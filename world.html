The Five Nations

    The heart of the fallen Galifar Empire. This five nations united under a single banner and forged a peace throughout the continent that lasted nearly a millennia. Even though they shared a banner, and many cultural similarities, they also held their own sense of individuality. As the most populous of the Khorvarian countries, most journeys start here, but by no means do they need to end here.

Khorvaire

    These other nations were once, and in one case technically still is, part of each of the Five Nations at their peak. Due to largely being annexed into the Empire rather than built from it, they each have more distinct cultures than the Five and tend to have higher populations of non-humans as well.

Beyond Khorvaire

    While the brunt of the fight took place on Khorvaire proper, nearly ever other nation in the world took part in the Last War, mostly as weapons dealers or as mercenaries. Most of these regions see a unifying nation or culture, so they tend to be much less diverse than Khorvaire, although with international trade opening back up, more mingling is commonplace compared to before.
