Eberron Timeline

Due to the mysterious origins of Eberron and the rise and fall of many civilizations, tracking history accurately has been difficult to say the least. Things become much clearer during the rule of Galifar Empire and from records by the Giants many generations before. Most history before that comes from artifacts found in ruins or through myths and legends passed down through the ages. This is a rough timeline of facts and commonly believed theories of Eberron's past. This is not exhaustive and may not be 100% correct, but in terms of what would be regarded as standard knowledge in setting. The Galifar calendar was established during the second dynasty and split in two eras. The years are first counted backwards, like BC and are 'Before Galifar' while years after the founding ar YK 'Year of the Kingdom'.

The Beginning

Unknown Years Ago

The three Great Progenitor Wyrms worked together to create the planes of existence, but Kyber betrayed and killed her brother Siberys, shattering his body which became the ring of crystals surrounding the planet. Eberron in revenge, coiled herself around her sister, becoming the world and Kyber becoming the extradimensional space underground. It is part of this myth that the blood that was spilt gave birth to the races that rose to dominance over the following eons.


The Age of Demons

~10,000,000 BG

The spawn of Kyber quickly dominate the land at the dawn of time. Devils, Rakshasa, and Night Hags rule various parts of the world. Dragons and mortals are under subjugation while the Coatl stay secluded in what would later become Sarlona.

~1,500,000 BG

Dragons discover the Dragon Prophecy and journey to Sarolona and meet the Coatls. They decide to join together to overthrow the Demon Lords.

~100,000 BG

Combining the psionic abilities of the Coatl and the arcane magic the Dragons, the forces tear through the demonic hordes, while the mortal races take cover in the most remote corners of the world and underground. After many centuries the War comes to a standstill. The majority of the Coatls sacrifice themselves to weaken and bind the remaining Lords and trap them within Kyber, creating the first iteration of the Silver Flame, as this point a world-wide force rather than a physical manifestation. The Dragons Seclude themselves in Argonnessen to study the Prophecy, and largely ignore the mortal races.


The Age of Giants

~80,000 BG

The Giants rise from the war and quickly take over most of the world, ruling from Xen'drik. They develop technology and magic that has been unrivaled even to this day. They experiment on the still primitive mortal races, leading directly to many of the subraces and their inherent magical abilities. The Giants make contact across several planes of existence.

~60,000 BG

The Dragons and Giants meet during this time, and seeing each other as contemporaries, teach each other magic, forming the first spells. Some of the subjugated begin to learn from observation. The Giants begin to lessen their hold across the globe, secluding themselves and furthering their studies of the planes and the arcane.

~40,000 BG

The Quori, denizens of Dal Quor launch an invasion against Eberron sparking a cataclysmic war across the world. Some Quori set a small enclave in Sarlona, now home to Humans. The mortals are able to hold their own against the extradimensional forces, being that most are concentrated against the Giants. Over time, the Giants develop a way to sever the link to Dal Quor completely. This either caused or was because of the destruction of the eponymous moon. In addition, around a third of Xen'drik was lost, leading to the separation the main land and the islands of Arenal. After this the land of Xen'drik became turbulent and shifted constantly, many Giants simply disappeared during this time as well. The remaining Giants were either slain by their former thrall and Dragons, now fearful of their power or driven into the depths of the now fluctuating continent.


Age of Monsters

~38,000 BG

With the Giants no longer interfering, the mortal races begin to develop culturally in their respective continents. Goblins claim central and southern Khorvaire and the Orcs claim western Khorvaire. The Lizardfolk and other reptilian races are pushed into southeastern Khorvaire, where they make contact with the newly arrived and previously unknown Dragonborn.

~25,000-10,000 BG

The Undying Court is founded in Arenal. The Orcs start developing druidic magic. During a minor ice age, Dwarves migrate from Frostfell to Khorvaire. The Goblins unite under the Dhakanni Empire and claim the remaining regions of Khorvaire. The eastern region of the Talenta Plains are independent due to the Halfling's bond with the dinosaurs of the area, while groups of Areni Elves found Valenar, fighting with the Goblins.

~8,000-4,000 BG

The Daelkyr of Xoriat invades through Kyber, primarily on Khorvaire, with the Goblin Empire largely holding them at bay. Small bands of Humans and Valenar Elves also fight against the Goblins during this time. The Dhakanni Empire falls, and Daelkyr forces begin to overrun the continent. The Gatekeeper Druids cast a great magic similar to the Giant's but are able to safely cut off access to Xoriat and seal many of the Daelkyr within Kyber.


Age of Kings

~3000-1500 BG

The first instances of dragonmarks are recorded and clans start to form around them. Humans begin arriving to Khorvaire and ally with the civilizations already there. Goblins are now much rarer and largely treated as second class citizens. The Five Nations are founded and peacefully co-exist for a time. Erandis Vol is born and house Vol is destroyed during this time period as well. The last remnants of the Goblin empire are destroyed and Karrnath attempts to take over the other nations.

~1300-500 BG

The Houses start to from from their clans. The remaining Quori from so long ago awaken and lead an assault on Sarlona. Some rebel against their leaders and bond with humans, forming the Kalashtar race. A second wave of humans immigrate to Khorvaire during this time. The War of the Mark takes place to eradicate aberrant dragonmarks. Sharn is founded.

~490-45 BG

The War of the Mark is ended after the death of Lord Tarkanan. Sharn is largely destroyed by Tarkanan and is rebuilt over the next few decades. Redia and Adar are founded in Sarlona. The Twelve is established. Io'lokar is founded in Argonnessen and is the first and only to be open to outside races. Galifar I is born.

~41-0 BG

The Border Wars between the Five Nations begins. Galifar I begins his campaign by allying with the Houses. The last of the dragonmarks is discovered. Galifar I manages to either unite or subjugate what will become the Galifar Empire. Maps are redrawn to accommodate these nations and several are renamed to their current names.

1-60 YK

Arcanix and its university is founded. Sharn is rebuilt. Galifar passes the throne to Cyre before passing away due to old age.

100-700 YK

The last of the Houses joins the Twelve. The Overlord Bel Salor breaks free from his confines and causes much destruction across Thrane. Tir Mirion and her Coatl companion sacrifice themselves to re-contain him, and reconstitute the Flame into its modern incarnation, the Church of the Silver Flame is born soon after. An army of monsters from Kyber lead by Medusas take claim in western Breland.

789-811 YK

The first sending stones are created and put into use. The Lightning Rail is developed and connects Flamekeep and Fairhaven. The first expeditions into Xen'drik take place. The Church of the Silver Flame claims control over Thrane. House Cannith takes the lead in developing new technologies. Daelkyr magic is discovered by researchers, who are quickly excomunicated from all magical institutions. King Jarot is born.

832-894 YK

The Lyccanthropathic Inquisition takes place over the next 50 years. King Jarot connects all capitols with Lightning Rails. Development of less expensive, mundane trains also develop. Houses begin expanding their employment beyond those with dragonmarks and relatives. King Jarot dies.


The Modern Era

896-918 YK

The Last War begins. The Order of the Emerald Claw launches its first attack on Sharn, damage is great, but Sharn recovers. Karrnath loses many of its soldiers after a famine and King Kaius I accepts the Blood of Vol as the official religion. Karrnath begins building its undead armies and the king disappears. Mror Holds declares its independence. Sharn is victim to yet another attack.

920-969 YK

Q'barra and Valenar are founded. The Eldeen Reaches claim independence and ally with the druids. Breland and Zilgaro become official allies and become one of the mightiest forces in the War. House Cannith develops the first Warforged. Harruc founds the Goblin nation of Darguun.

970-990 YK

House Phialan splits. The Order of the Emerald Claw re-emerges and is declared a terrorist cell by Karrnath. The Daughters of Sora Kell take over the Droaam region of Breland. The first airships are developed.

991-1006 YK

Cyre is lost and becomes the Mournlands. The War ends and the Treaty of Thronehold redraws maps to their current state. Droaam is the only country left unrecognized, but no effort is made to take back the land. The Warforged are granted full rights as sapient beings and the Cannith Creation Forges are ordered to be destroyed. House Cannith is split into three branches. The city of New Cyre is founded. Reports of disapearences and deaths begin to take place across Khorvaire, blame is put on cults of the Dragon Below. Your adventure begins.
