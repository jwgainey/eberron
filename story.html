Chapter 1

The end of the Last War brought many changes. Many are left without purpose or the proper skill set to adjust to a civilian life. Fortunately for them, the world is still full of dangers, and with international trade and exploration on the rise, help with very dangerous work is in high demand. In particular, disappearances and deaths in remote areas across Khorvaire seem to be on the rise. This could be just an increase in attention being paid due to the peace, but some are wary to Eberron's tendency to have world-changing events at the end of empires. Either way, one small mining town called Golddust, located in a tucked away valley in Breland has attracted the attention of various agencies. Even though it is on the edge of the wilds, and supported by dangerous work, deaths and disappearances there seem a bit too frequent. at the very least this may be a lead to find something to do for the time being.

Why do you find yourself coming to this rather unremarkable town? To expose a cover-up from negligent company? To break a curse or exterminate a deadly creature? Maybe you simply want the job that just opened up, there seems to be quite a few.
